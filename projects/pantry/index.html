Pantry App

June 2026 - Access at pantry.thomasnewman.ca

This was my first full-stack project, which included a backend powered by Supabase, and payment processing using Stripe. It's an app called Pantry, and it helps you keep track of what you have in your pantry from anywhere.

My goal for this was to give it a mature feeling, its fast and snappy, and looks professional. It makes uses of a user database, that they can add to whenever they want to create an item. After the users add their items, all of the information that was entered is saved to the database, and when you search the database, you can find those items again.

The premium subscription gives you access to unlimited items in your pantry, while the free version limits you to 50 entries (more than enough for a single person, but families might want premium). The premium features include smart recipes (for quickly finding something you can make with what you've got) & smart grocery lists (for quickly finding items you're missing), as well as family pantry sync, folders, etc. This project is very much still in progress, so many features are not available yet or not fully implemented.

Features

  • Powered by Supabase and Stripe on Next.JS
  • Users can sign up and make an account using the traditional method but also Sign In with Google
  • Users can easily add items to the public database, featuring any nutrition information available on the label
    • Premium Features
  • Smart grocery list (WIP)
  • Smart recipe generator (WIP)
  • Family Pantry (WIP)
  • Mobile Support planned
